Your right to loan a car after an accident


Most people don't know that if you are not at-fault in a car accident, and more than one car is involved, you may have the right to loan a car with the costs covered by the at-fault party.




If you qualify for a car with Right2Drive, you will not be liable for the rental costs provided you assist us in the recovery of costs from the at-fault party and act in accordance with our terms and conditions.

Will you be stuck with the costs of the loan car?

Once your car has been repaired or you have been notified it is a total loss, we will invoice at-fault party for payment of the loan car on your behalf.


If the at-fault party doesn't pay the invoice we may commence legal procedings in your name against the insurer. If this happens we may require your assistance, such as providing us with a statement and in some instances attending court as a witness. Please note over 98% of our customers are not required to attend court.


Provided you are not at fault, fulfil your obligations and otherwise act in accordance with the terms and conditions of our aggrement, we will not hold you liable for the loan car costs, even of insurers come to a different decisions later on.
